About Amarachi Okereke - Counsellor

With over six years of experience in life coaching, relationship coaching, counselling, couples therapy, academic guidance, and depression therapy across Nigeria, Amarachi Okereke is dedicated to supporting individuals, couples, and groups in Warri and all over the world.

As the CEO of Amiable Counselling Consultancy, she offers both virtual and physical services, including individual counselling/therapy sessions and group trainings.
Her approach blends Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Rational Emotive Behavioral Therapy (REBT), mindfulness techniques, and person-centered counseling to foster a safe, culturally sensitive space for healing and growth. She is a Certified Marriage and Family Christian Counselor, Certified Relationship Coach and Guidance Counselor; and an active member of CASSON (Counselling Association of Nigeria)

Amiable Counselling Consultancy currently serves clients in Nigeria, UK, USA, Ghana, Gambia, Canada, and Kenya.
